Chicken Adobo

A popular Filipino dish made with chicken simmered in a mixture of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices. It's flavorful and perfect with rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp whole peppercorns
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, marinate chicken in soy sauce for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pot over medium heat. Add onions and garlic, and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add marinated chicken and cook until browned.
  4. Add vinegar and let it simmer for 5 minutes without stirring.
  5. Add water, bay leaf, and peppercorns. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes or until chicken is tender.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot with rice.
