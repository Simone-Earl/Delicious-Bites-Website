Beef Stroganoff

A creamy and savory beef stroganoff with tender strips of beef and mushrooms in a rich sauce. Serve over noodles or rice for a hearty meal.

Ingredients

  • 500g beef sirloin, sliced into strips
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 200g mushrooms, sliced
  • 200ml beef broth
  • 200ml sour cream
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked egg noodles or rice for serving

Instructions

  1. In a pan, melt butter and cook beef strips until browned. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same pan, saut√© onion, garlic, and mushrooms until soft.
  3. Sprinkle flour over the vegetables and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add beef broth, bring to a simmer, then return beef to the pan.
  5. Stir in sour cream and cook until the sauce is heated through. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve over egg noodles or rice.
