Egg Sandwich

An easy and delicious egg sandwich, perfect for a quick breakfast or lunch. Creamy egg filling with your choice of fresh vegetables and seasonings, all sandwiched between slices of bread.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 4 slices of bread (white, whole wheat, or your choice)
  • 2 leaves of lettuce (optional)
  • 1 tomato, sliced (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil eggs in a pot of water for 10 minutes. Remove from heat, drain, and cool in ice water. Peel and chop the eggs.
  2. In a bowl, mix mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper. Add chopped eggs, celery, and green onions. Stir until well combined.
  3. Toast the bread slices if desired. Spread egg mixture evenly on two slices of bread.
  4. Top with lettuce and tomato if using. Place the remaining bread slices on top to form sandwiches.
  5. Cut sandwiches in half and serve immediately, or wrap and take with you for a lunch on the go.
