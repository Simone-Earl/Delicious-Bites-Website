Beef Stir-Fry

This Beef Stir-Fry is a quick and delicious dish featuring tender beef strips and crisp vegetables, all coated in a savory sauce. Perfect for a busy weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 500g beef sirloin or flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup snap peas
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water (for thickening)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat.
  2. Add beef slices and cook until browned and cooked through, about 3-4 minutes. Remove beef from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add onions and garlic. Cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  4. Add bell pepper, broccoli, and snap peas. Stir-fry until vegetables are tender-crisp, about 3-4 minutes.
  5. Return beef to the skillet and add soy sauce, oyster sauce, and hoisin sauce. Stir well to combine.
  6. Stir in the cornstarch mixture and cook for another 1-2 minutes, until the sauce has thickened.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot over cooked rice or noodles.
