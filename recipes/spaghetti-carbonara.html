Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. It's creamy, delicious, and perfect for a comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino cheese
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  2. In a pan, cook the pancetta until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, mix the eggs and cheeses.
  4. Combine the spaghetti with the pancetta, then remove from heat and mix in the egg mixture.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste and serve immediately.
