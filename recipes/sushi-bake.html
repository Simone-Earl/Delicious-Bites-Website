Sushi Bake

Sushi Bake is a delicious and easy-to-make dish inspired by sushi rolls, but in a convenient casserole form. It's a great party dish or family meal, combining sushi rice, seafood, and creamy sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi rice, cooked and seasoned with rice vinegar
  • 1 lb imitation crab or cooked seafood of choice, chopped
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup sriracha or other hot sauce (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese, softened
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 1 sheet nori (seaweed), shredded (optional, for garnish)
  • Sliced cucumber or avocado for topping (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Lightly grease a baking dish.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the mayonnaise, sriracha, and cream cheese until smooth.
  3. Fold in the chopped seafood and green onions.
  4. Spread the cooked sushi rice evenly in the bottom of the prepared baking dish.
  5. Spread the seafood mixture over the rice, smoothing it out with a spatula.
  6. Sprinkle sesame seeds on top.
  7. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly.
  8. Garnish with shredded nori, cucumber, or avocado if desired. Serve warm with soy sauce for dipping.
