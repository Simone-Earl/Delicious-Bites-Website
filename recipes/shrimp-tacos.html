Shrimp Tacos

Flavorful shrimp seasoned with spices and served in soft tortillas with fresh toppings. A quick and delicious taco recipe that's perfect for any day of the week.

Ingredients

  • 400g shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 8 small tortillas
  • Shredded lettuce, chopped tomatoes, and diced avocado for topping
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, toss shrimp with olive oil, paprika, cumin, garlic powder, and chili powder.
  2. Cook shrimp in a pan over medium-high heat until pink and cooked through.
  3. Warm tortillas and fill with shrimp and your choice of toppings.
  4. Serve with lime wedges on the side.
