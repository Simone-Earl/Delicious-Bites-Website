Sweet Potato Bites

Deliciously crispy and tender sweet potato bites, perfect as an appetizer or a side dish. They are seasoned and baked to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 2 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into bite-sized cubes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, toss sweet potato cubes with olive oil, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, cumin, salt, and pepper until evenly coated.
  3. Spread the sweet potato cubes in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until sweet potatoes are crispy on the outside and tender inside, turning once halfway through cooking.
  5. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired, and serve warm.
