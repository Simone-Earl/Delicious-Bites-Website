Mushroom Risotto

A creamy risotto with earthy mushrooms and Parmesan cheese. A comforting and elegant dish perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 200g mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a pan, heat olive oil and cook mushrooms until browned. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same pan, cook onion and garlic until translucent.
  3. Add Arborio rice and cook for 1-2 minutes until slightly toasted.
  4. Deglaze with white wine and stir until absorbed.
  5. Add broth gradually, stirring frequently, until rice is cooked and creamy.
  6. Stir in cooked mushrooms, Parmesan cheese, and butter. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Serve immediately.
